1. CORE SYSTEMS & FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[LOBBY SYSTEM]
The server features a dedicated Lobby world that serves as the central hub.
- Protection: The Lobby is fully protected. No damage, hunger, PVP, or block breaking allowed.
- Launchpads: Step on a Golden Pressure Plate to be launched into the air!
- Weather: Always clear weather in the lobby.
- Interaction: Doors, trapdoors, and chests are locked to prevent tampering.

[RTP (RANDOM TELEPORT)]
- Command: /rtp [world]
- Function: Teleports you to a random location in the survival world to start your adventure.
- Logic: Finds a safe location (not water/lava) within a configurable radius.
- Restriction: Cannot be used directly in the Lobby without specifying a world.

[COMBAT SYSTEM]
- Combat Tag: Engaging in PVP tags you for 20 seconds.
- Restrictions: While tagged, you cannot use specific commands (e.g., /spawn, /home).
- Combat Logging: If you disconnect while tagged, you will instantly die, lose your items, 
  and a lightning strike will mark your death spot. A broadcast will shame the combat logger!
- Action Bar: A timer shows exactly how long you are tagged.

[ECONOMY & SHOP]
- Currency: Dollars ($)
- Shop: A GUI-based shop to buy and sell items.
- Sell Hand: Quickly sell the item in your hand with /sell.
- Auction House: Players can list items for sale to others.
  - Sorting: Sort by Newest, Oldest, Price (Low/High).
  - Expiry: Auctions expire after a set time (items return to owner).
- Balance: Check your money with /balance.

[TEAMS]
- Create your own team and invite friends!
- Prefix: Team names may appear in chat or scoreboards (depending on config).
- Management: Invite, Kick, and Disband options available.

3. CUSTOM SPAWNERS & LOOT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Spawners on SMP369 are upgraded! They have internal storage for XP and Loot, 
and custom drops for specific mobs.

[MECHANICS]
- Stacking: Spawners can be stacked to increase efficiency.
- Storage: Spawners automatically collect XP and Loot. You must click them to collect.
- Upgrades: Spawner speed increases with your Rank!

[CUSTOM DROPS]

- Skeleton: Bone + Arrow

4. COMMAND REFERENCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[GENERAL]
/spawn          - Teleport to the world spawn.
/lobby          - Teleport to the lobby.
/rtp            - Randomly teleport in the survival world.
/tpa <player>   - Request to teleport to a player.
/tpahere <player> - Request a player to teleport to you.
/tpaccept       - Accept a teleport request.
/tpdeny         - Deny a teleport request.
/back           - Teleport to your last location (Death/TP).

[HOMES]
/sethome [name] - Set a home at your current location.
/home [name]    - Teleport to a specific home.
/delhome [name] - Delete a home.
/homes          - List all your homes (GUI).

[ECONOMY]
/balance        - Check your current balance.
/pay <player> <amount> - Send money to another player.
/shop           - Open the server shop.
/sell           - Sell the item in your hand to the server.
/ah             - Open the Auction House.
/ah sell <price> - Sell the item in your hand on the Auction House.

[TEAMS]
/team create <name> - Create a new team.
/team invite <player> - Invite a player to your team.
/team join <name>   - Join a team (requires invite).
/team leave         - Leave your current team.
/team info [name]   - View team members and info.

[RANKS]
/rank           - Open the Rank Upgrade GUI.
/rank set <player> <rank> - (Admin) Set a player's rank.

[ADMIN]
/smp369 reload  - Reload the plugin configuration.
/setspawn       - Set the global spawn point.
/setlobby       - Set the lobby spawn point.
/gamemode <mode> - Change gamemode.
